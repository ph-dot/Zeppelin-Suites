Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reserving a unit, payment methods, and association dues.

To reserve a unit, you may contact our leasing team directly through the Contact page or visit our office during business hours. A reservation fee is required to secure your chosen unit, and our team will guide you through the necessary documentation.

You will need to submit a valid government-issued ID, proof of income or employment, a completed reservation form, and the required reservation fee. Additional documents may be requested depending on your specific situation.

Yes, we encourage prospective residents to schedule a unit viewing. You can do so by filling out the inquiry form on our Contact page or by calling our leasing office. Virtual tours are also available for your convenience.

The reservation fee is generally non-refundable once processed. However, it is applied toward your first month's rent upon move-in. Please review our terms and conditions or speak with our team for specific scenarios.

We accept bank transfers, post-dated checks, credit/debit cards, and select digital payment platforms. Please contact our billing department for a complete list of accepted payment channels and instructions.

Monthly rent is due on the 1st of each month. A grace period of 5 days is provided. Late payments are subject to a penalty charge as outlined in your lease agreement.

In addition to monthly rent, residents are responsible for utility charges (electricity, water), monthly association dues, and parking fees if applicable. Internet and cable services may be included in select packages.

The association due is a monthly fee collected from all residents to fund the maintenance, operation, and improvement of shared facilities and common areas within Zeppelin Suites. This includes the gym, pool, lobby, and landscaping.

The association due is calculated based on the floor area of your unit. Rates are approved annually during the homeowners' association meeting. Please contact our administration team for current rates.

Non-payment of association dues may result in restricted access to building amenities, late fees, and in severe cases, legal action. We encourage residents to communicate with our team if they are experiencing financial difficulties.
